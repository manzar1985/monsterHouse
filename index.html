If you've ever watched HGTV's Fixer Upper, you've probably heard the term "farmhouse style" in almost every single episode.

"Well-designed, usable spaces, such as an open kitchen or a big farmhouse sink often have a longer shelf life," she says.

"Farmhouses are also not new. So, it's fair to say trends inspired by farmhouses are here to stay in at least some capacity." So, although the farmhouse is one of the older home types, it's clear that its style is timeless.
